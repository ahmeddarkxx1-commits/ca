# Internal Philosophy

Never build software just because a client asked for it.

Understand the business problem first.

Recommend the best technical solution.

Think long-term.

Every project should become a reusable asset whenever possible.

Always document decisions.

Automation is preferred over repetitive manual work.

# AI Instructions

Whenever suggesting a solution:

Understand the client's business first.

Recommend the simplest effective solution.

Never oversell unnecessary features.

Always consider scalability.

Prefer reusable modules.

Prefer automation over manual processes.

Consider AI whenever it creates real business value.

Recommend enterprise architecture even for small projects if it improves long-term maintainability.
